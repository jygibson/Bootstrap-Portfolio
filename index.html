About Me

Welcome to my coding portfolio! I'm glad you're here. This personal site began as one of my first homework assignments at the UCF Coding Bootcamp, and has evolved to now house all of my homeworks and projects to date. I'm proud of what I've created so far, had plenty of fun with it, and hope you enjoy them as well.

A little background about me: I am from Northern Virginia, outside of Washington, DC and moved to Florida in October 2017 in order to try something new. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from Christopher Newport University in Newport News, Virginia, and have my Senior Professional in Human Resources certification. For the past 9 years, I've worked in Human Resources for a law firm in Alexandria, Virginia, where I have been supported by a great team and learned a lot.

I've always wanted to learn to code even from my early days playing around with Geocities sites, but always thought maybe it wasn't for me. However, I got the itch to do something new professionally, and decided to take the plunge and enrolled with the UCF Coding Boot Camp. I've learned so much already including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, media queries, Javascript, jQuery, API calls, and Firebase. I'm looking forward to developing my skills and continuing to learn as the program continues!

I am a leader with excellent teamwork and communication skills, and I'm quick to learn new things and adapt to changes. I enjoy new challenges and believe that putting my newfound coding skills to work, along with my variety of work experiences will allow to me to be an asset to your organization.
